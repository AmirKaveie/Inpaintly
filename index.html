Frequently Asked Questions

How do I use Inpaintly's in-painting and mask tools?
Draw a mask over any area you want to edit using our freehand or shape preset tools, then enter a text prompt describing what you want. Inpaintly will generate natural, high-quality results instantly!
How do I remove objects from my photos?
Simply draw a mask over the object you want to remove (people, logos, blemishes, etc.) and Inpaintly will seamlessly fill the area with matching background details.
Can I add new objects to my photos?
Yes! Mask the area where you want to add something, then use a custom text prompt like "sunset beach," "vintage car," or "colorful flowers" to add new content with perfect blending.
What are text prompts and how do I use them?
Text prompts let you describe exactly what you want Inpaintly to create. Be specific - instead of "tree," try "tall oak tree with green leaves" for better results.
Can I see my edits before applying them?
Absolutely! Inpaintly shows live previews so you can see your edits update in real time before committing to changes.
What file formats are supported?
Inpaintly supports all common image formats including JPEG, PNG, and HEIC photos from your camera roll. Export and share your creations in high resolution.
Can I undo my changes if I make a mistake?
Yes! Inpaintly includes instant undo and redo features so you can experiment fearlessly and try different edits without worry.
How do I cancel my subscription?
Subscriptions are managed through your Apple ID settings. Go to Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions, find Inpaintly, and choose your preferred option.
Why isn't my in-painting working as expected?
For best results, try being more specific with your text prompts and ensure you've masked the area precisely. Good original photo quality and lighting also help achieve better in-painting results.
Can I restore my original photo?
Yes! Inpaintly always preserves your original photo. You can use the undo feature or access the original version through the app's editing history.
Is my data private and secure?
Absolutely. Your photos are processed securely and we follow strict privacy guidelines. Check our Privacy Policy for full details on how we protect your information.
The app is crashing or not working properly
Try restarting the app or rebooting your device. If issues persist, please contact our support team with details about your device model and iOS version.
